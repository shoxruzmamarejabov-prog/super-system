FLOW CHART TOOLS

Stickies

Press “S” to quickly add a sticky
to the canvas.

Rectangles

Press “R” to quickly place a
rectangle onto the canvas. Other
shapes can be selected from the
toolbar or swapped from the
shape menu.

Stickers

Quickly access fun stickers to
add to your brainstorm. If you
have component libraries in
Figma, you can also add
them here. Find stickers and
components in the toolbox.
